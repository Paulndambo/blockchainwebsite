    Tokenizing Public Infrastructure

    XYZ is a financial technology company applying innovative approach to bridging infrastructure gap in Africa using blockchain. With the continent requiring $170 billion a year for infrastructure, there is a need to attract private capital and blockchain-based tokenization offers the best way possible to achieve that. We are building a blockchain technology specifically for infrastructure asset tokenization to help close the continent’s infrastructure needs.
